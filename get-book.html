Frequently Asked Questions

Is this book suitable for all ages?

This memoir is written for adult women and deals with mature themes including toxic relationships, trauma recovery, and personal transformation. We recommend it for readers 18 and older.

How is this different from other self-help books?

This isn't traditional self-help—it's a raw, honest memoir with practical tools woven throughout. The focus is on body-based healing and authentic transformation rather than quick fixes or surface-level advice.

Can I read this if I'm still in a difficult situation?

Yes, but please prioritize your safety and well-being. This book can be a source of strength and validation, but it's not a substitute for professional support when needed.
